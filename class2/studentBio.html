Student Bio

Wilson Calil

Wilson Calil is a senior marketing executive with more than 15 years’ experience working with Fortune 500 companies in product marketing, business intelligence, consumer insight, branding and digital marketing, heading up business development projects in China, Brazil and Chile and leading marketing teams in nine major Latin American countries and territories.

As an industry leader in product marketing, Wilson developed and launched more than a hundred products and dozens of consumer research studies. While directing three product lines in Brazil, he spearheaded the team that tripled net sales and EBIT for his division, earning more than $200M USD in revenues and $32M USD in profits.

In 2015, Wilson was named Latin America’s Top Marketer by the New York-based digital magazine Portada-Online and was selected as one of the Top Fifty Latin American Marketers by The Internationalist magazine.

Wilson has just relocated to Houston from Miami and is currently working on a disruptive new digital platform to develop more effective ways of recruiting and hiring marketing professionals and agencies.
